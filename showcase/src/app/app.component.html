  • SimpleLock

    An (outdated) Android library that simplifies the use of pin codes on your app. It helps you to create a pin screen and store the saved pin hashed. You can have as many different pins for your app as you want, you can change the style and configure callbacks for pin creation or pin entering.

    See more info on github.

  • Family Care (WIP)

    It's an Android App that will track your family members state. It is focused on help you with the care of older people, your parents or grandparents as well as people with neurodegenerative diseases or cognitive diseases. The main purpose is to make it easier for you to detect if something goes wrong and solve that problem.

    See the project on github.
